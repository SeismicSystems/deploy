## Custody Solutions

### Fireblocks

Native gas token support with Fireblocks is planned.

### General Custody

Since Seismic is EVM-compatible, any custody solution supporting Ethereum should work with standard (non-shielded) features. For shielded token custody, please contact the team for specialized guidance.

For institutional custody inquiries, contact c@seismic.systems.
