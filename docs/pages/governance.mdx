### Staking

**Current status**: Information about staking, delegation, and validation is not yet finalized.

**What we know**:

### Governance

**Current status**: Governance mechanisms have not been publicly specified yet.

Governance details (voting mechanisms, parameter changes, proposals) will be announced as the network matures.

### Actions at Network Launch

**At launch**, participants should be prepared for:
- **Transfers**: Sending and receiving the native token
- **Contract interactions**: Deploying and interacting with smart contracts
- **Token holdings**: Custody of native assets and potentially SRC20 tokens

**Future participation** may include:
- Staking and delegation (mechanisms TBD)
- Governance voting (mechanisms TBD)
- Validator operations (requirements TBD)

### Vesting Schedules

**Status**: Vesting schedule details have not been publicly disclosed. For institutional inquiries, contact the team directly for information about:
- Vesting start/end dates and unlock frequency
- On-chain vs off-chain vesting implementation
- Whether locked tokens can be used for staking or governance
- Whether vested tokens need to be claimed or are automatically distributed
